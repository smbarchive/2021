The SMB2021 Career Fair will be held exclusively in Sococo:

  • Meet with Industry Sponsors: Check out their Sococo booths on the 'SMB Welcome/Industry' floor.
  • Looking for a job or internship? Check out job postings on the 'SMB Social/Career Fair' floor.
  • Looking to hire? Check out the CVs submitted by registrants on the 'SMB Social/Career Fair' floor

The event will be held Thursday June 17 at 01:00pm (PDT), Thursday June 17 at 09:00pm (BST), or Friday June 18 at 05:00am (KST).
