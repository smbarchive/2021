Q&A with NSF Mathematical Biology Program Directors

You are invited to meet the Program Directors of the Mathematical Biology program in the NSF Division of Mathematical Sciences (DMS) for a discussion of NSF funding opportunities relevant to the Mathematical Biology community. They will address any questions you may have regarding the development of NSF proposals and/or suggestions for new NSF programs in Math Biology. The directors are listed below.

  • Junping Wang, jwang@nsf.gov
  • Zhilan Feng, zfeng@nsf.gov

There will be two sessions:

  • 11:00am PDT on Monday 14th of June
  • 7:45am PDT on Tuesday 15th of June
