Discussions Celebrating and Building Diversity in Mathematical Biology: Where do we want to go from here?

Thursday, June 17 at 1-2PM PDT

The goal of our work in diversity, equity and inclusion is about cultivating spaces in which all voices are heard, valued and validated. In this closing session, we reflect on the shape of our professional spaces and how to direct our energies forward beyond SMB 2021 both within the Society of Mathematical Biology and the spaces we each touch through our unique spheres of influence.
