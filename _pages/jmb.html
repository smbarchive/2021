Journal of Mathematical Biology Info Session

The Editors of the Journal of Mathematical Biology will present a special session which will cover how to submit, the usual pitfalls to avoid, what sort of papers the journal is interested in receiving. This session is aimed at prospective authors.

There will be two sessions:

  • 7:45am on Wednesday June 16 (PDT), 03:45pm on Wednesday June 16 (BST), 11:45pm on Thursday June 17 (KST)
  • 11:00am on Thursday June 17 (PDT), 07:00pm on Thursday June 17 (BST), 03:00am on Friday June 18 (KST)
