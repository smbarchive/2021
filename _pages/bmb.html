Bulletin of Mathematical Biology Info Session

The Editors-in-chief of the Bulletin of Mathematical Biology will present a special session which will cover how to submit to the Bulletin, the usual pitfalls to avoid, what sort of papers the journal is interested in receiving. This session is aimed at prospective authors for the society's flagship journal.

Submission guidelines for the journal are found here.

There will be two sessions:

  • 11:00am on Tuesday June 15 (PDT), 07:00pm on Tuesday June 15 (BST), 03:00am on Wednesday June 16 (KST)
  • 7:45am on Thursday June 17 (PDT), 03:45pm on Thursday June 17 (BST), 11:45pm on Friday June 18 (KST)
