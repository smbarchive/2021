Book Publishing @ Springer

Speaker: Elena Griniari - Senior Editor, Mathematics

05:45am on Thursday June 17 (PDT), 01:45pm on Thursday June 17 (BST), 09:45pm on Thursday June 17 (KST)

Brief Description:

This session is devoted to a discussion of the book publishing process, covering topics such as the substantial difference between journals and books publishing, manuscript submission, peer-review process and acceptance and the role of the Publisher in supporting authors throughout the process.

The target audience is young researchers and experienced authors who wish to be guided or updated on the subject.

Elena Griniari will be also glad to provide info on specific tools and resources offered by Springer for free, and answer your questions.
